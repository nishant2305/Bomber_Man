Instructions :
    You can move in all 8 directions.
    W - Move up
    S - Move down
    A - Move left
    D - Move right
    Q - Move diagonally up left
    Z - Move diagonally down left
    E - Move diagonally up right
    C - Move diagonally down right
    -> The player can not move to a position if there is a Wall or Brick -> If there is a Villain where the player moves. The player dies -> If there is a Key where the player moves. The player wins the game
    -> The player can plant a bomb to destroy Bricks and Villains.
    X - Bomb
